A few things 🙋🏻 clients normally ask me:

How much do you charge?
Generally I do hourly work for $30/hr. I actually prefer project pay vs hourly pay as it is usually a better deal for all parties involved. All in all I'm flexible with pay. Best way to find out is to have a discussion with me about what you want or need.
How did you get started?
I entered into the e-commerce field in 2014 and have since learned a tremendous amount about the industry. I have moved towards web development and e-commerce consulting as I could see over the last couple of years how much more opportunity is available there.
For me, I actually enjoy seeing others succeed more than seeing myself succeed. I enjoy being part of the process of creating others' dreams.
Do you work with any platform besides Shopify?
I do not have enough experience in other platforms to say with confidence that I can help. However, the same process I took to learn Shopify is reusable for other platforms so I am confident that I would be able to learn them if need be. I chose Shopify because it seems to be the best platform available for e-commerce.
What are you turn around times?
It depends on the project. This is another reason I value communication so much. If I don't take the time to  understand what you want I may under or overestimate the time it will take me to do something. As was stated before if you have specific questions email me below.
